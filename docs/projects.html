I've learned a plethora of concepts during my tenure in the DIGIT program including XML, HTML, XPath, Regex, XSLT, with a touch of Javascript and Schematron. The culmination of this knowledge is illustrated in the following projects.

Morrowind Librarium

This project was completed during the fall 2020 semester by myself and two other students. Here you'll find a comprehensive analysis of books exclusive to the Bethesda published game "The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind". I performed XML markup and constructed a RelaxNG schema for this project.

Dharma Bums Encoded

This project was also completed during the fall 2020 semester by myself and one other student. Here you'll find an analysis of a manuscript/published edition of a novel by Jack Kerouac titled "The Dharma Bums". For this project I performed XML markup, constructed a RelaxNG schema, and made a fair bit of XSLT for this project.
